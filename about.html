Welcome to the future of sustainable dining

At GreenGold, we are proud to offer an innovative range of eco-friendly tableware crafted from sugarcane bagasse — a genuinely environmentally conscious alternative to traditional plastic and paper-based tableware.

  • Eco-Friendly — Our tableware is biodegradable and compostable, breaking down naturally without harming the environment.
  • Durable & Sturdy — Designed to withstand both hot and cold foods, our tableware is microwave and freezer safe, providing convenience without compromising on quality.
  • Chemical-Free — Free from harmful chemicals and toxins, our products are safe for both you and the environment.
  • Versatile & Stylish — Available in a variety of shapes and sizes, our tableware is perfect for any occasion, from casual picnics to elegant events.

A better substitute, by design

Being a waste product of sugarcane, items made from bagasse are a relatively better sustainable substitute than conventional non-biodegradable, non-renewable disposables like plastic and styrofoam. In general, the key is to ensure they're sustainably and responsibly produced — which is exactly how we manufacture.

Renewable by nature

Every piece begins as leftover sugarcane fibre — a resource that's replenished every harvest, not extracted from the earth.

The Bagasse Process

The journey from a field of swaying sugarcane to a sustainable dish begins by gathering the leftover bagasse fibres after harvest — refined into sturdy sheets, molded by heat into tableware, and returned to a compost heap once your meal is finished. A cycle that nourishes the soil while protecting our world.
