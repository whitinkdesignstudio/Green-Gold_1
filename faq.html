Products & Materials

What are GreenGold products made from?

+

Every GreenGold product is made from sugarcane bagasse — the natural fibre left over after sugarcane is pressed for juice. It's pressed into sheets and thermoformed into tableware, giving you a renewable alternative to plastic and paper-based disposables.

Are your products microwave and freezer safe?

+

Yes. GreenGold tableware is microwave-safe and freezer-safe, so it can go from storage to reheating without switching containers.

Are the products food-grade and safe for direct food contact?

+

Yes. Our tableware is manufactured to be food-grade and toxin-free, and is sturdy, leak-resistant and oil-resistant — suitable for both hot and cold food service.

What product range do you manufacture?

+

Our current range includes:

  • Round Plates & Compartment Round Plates
  • Square Compartment Plates / Meal Trays
  • Bowls & Dona
  • Clamshells
  • Lids & Glass
  • Takeout Containers (rectangular & round)

New SKUs are added regularly — get in touch if you need a size or shape not listed on our products page.

Are GreenGold products compostable?

+

Yes — our bagasse tableware is designed to be compostable and biodegradable under composting conditions, making it a genuine alternative to conventional disposable plastic products for food-service and institutional use.

Ordering & Pricing

How do I get pricing for a product?

+

We work on a request-for-quotation (RFQ) basis rather than a public price list. Send us your product, size and volume requirement via WhatsApp or the enquiry form, and our team will get back with a formal quote.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

+

For export orders, our MOQ is a 20' or 40' container load. Products can be mixed within a single container, provided each individual product meets its own carton MOQ (typically 500 pcs/carton, as listed on our products page).

What are your payment terms?

+

Standard terms are 30% advance payment, with the balance 70% payable against the Proforma Invoice (PI) before dispatch.

Can I request a sample before placing a bulk order?

+

Yes. We welcome sample requests from prospective bulk buyers so you can evaluate quality, strength and finish before committing to a full order.

Do you offer a downloadable product catalogue?

+

Yes — product-wise catalogues and specification sheets (sizes, weights, packing/carton details) are available for download, alongside our company profile.

Export & Shipping

Which countries do you currently export to?

+

We currently export to the USA, UK, UAE, Mauritius and Australia — with active expansion planned across North America, Europe and the wider Middle East.

Which ports do you export from?

+

We primarily export from Mundra and Nhava Sheva ports in Gujarat/Maharashtra, India.

What Incoterms do you offer?

+

We can quote on EXW, FOB or CIF terms depending on your logistics preference — let us know what works for your shipment when requesting a quote.

Can I mix multiple products in a single container?

+

Yes. Buyers are not required to fill a container with a single product — you can mix categories within one shipment, as long as each product meets its own carton-level MOQ.

What export documentation and certifications do you provide?

+

A Certificate of Origin and other essential shipment documents are provided as standard. Any additional certification or documentation requirement can be catered to, subject to confirmation at the time of order finalisation.

OEM & Private Label

Do you offer OEM / private-label manufacturing?

+

Yes. OEM and private-label manufacturing is a core focus area for GreenGold — we support importers, distributors and brands who want products under their own identity.

What customization options are available?

+

We can support customized product specifications, packaging, branding, labeling and compliance requirements based on your target market. Share your requirement and our team will confirm exact scope and feasibility.

What is the MOQ and lead time for OEM / private-label orders?

+

OEM/private-label orders typically require an MOQ of 100,000 pieces per SKU, with a lead time of 30–45 days from artwork approval, advance payment, and final order confirmation.
